# Use in Full Screen Mode on iPhone and Android

For the best experience, launch Tripadvancer directly from your home screen to enjoy full-screen mode on your device.

#### How to Install on iPhone

1. Open Safari and go to https://www.tripadvancer.com.
2. Tap the "Share" button.
3. Select "Add to Home Screen".
4. Tap the Tripadvancer icon on your home screen to launch the app.

#### How to Install on Android

1. Open Chrome and go to https://www.tripadvancer.com.
2. Tap the menu button (three dots) in the top-right corner.
3. Select "Add to Home Screen".
4. Tap the Tripadvancer icon on your home screen to launch the app.

By using Tripadvancer in full-screen mode, you can take advantage of a more immersive and user-friendly interface. This makes it easier to browse and explore destinations, read reviews, and plan your trips without any distractions.
